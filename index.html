Home // 01

Your business knows where it’s going. But something about the technology supporting it doesn’t feel right.

That feeling is often right. It’s where Purple Swan does its best work - helping you build technology that you can trust, and that your business is counting on. An experienced technology executive on your side of the table before the decisions are made.

Technology leadership. Done for you.

In Practice // 02

Where the work usually begins. Here are a few scenarios where Purple Swan will help you:

  1. 02-A

    You’ve set the strategy. You need to know whether the technology underneath can carry it.

    We will help you with an independent read of the tech structure, the team that’s enabling it and the plan to make it happen, assisting you to get clarity that you can take to leaders, owners or the board.

  2. 02-B

    A major technology investment, on the table or already underway and not going to plan. You want it tested by someone with nothing riding on the answer.

    Where it will deliver, where it won’t, and the case for changing course if that’s the honest answer.

  3. 02-C

    Investors, a board or a regulator are about to look hard at the technology function. You want to see it the way they will, before they do.

    Purple Swan will help you get that perspective, then help you to close the gaps, on your terms.

  4. 02-D

    The business is growing faster than the technology was built for. Tech shouldn’t be the thing that holds you back.

    Strengthening the people, processes and technology, in that order, so that your tech paces your business instead of lagging behind.

  5. 02-E

    There’s a consequential technology decision to make, and no one inside is positioned to own it.

    Purple Swan will step in, carry the load, and keep the technology answerable to your commercial reality.

Close enough to the technology to read it, far enough away to keep perspective.
